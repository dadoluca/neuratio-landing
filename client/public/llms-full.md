## Product

Neuratio is an AI-powered ticketing platform purpose-built for machinery manufacturers. It sits between the manufacturer's customers and their technical support team, automating the resolution of repetitive requests and routing complex ones to the right specialist.

**Core capabilities:**
- Analyzes incoming technical support requests
- Automatically responds to common/repetitive issues using the manufacturer's documentation
- Suggests AI-generated answers for technicians on complex tickets
- Escalates issues that require human expertise — with full context pre-compiled
- Maintains and grows a centralized knowledge base
- Operates 24/7 in multiple languages

---

## Problems Solved

Neuratio addresses six critical pain points for machinery manufacturers:

1. **Endless email chains** — Repetitive manual requests create disorganized, untracked communication between customers and support teams.
2. **Time zone challenges** — Global customer bases require round-the-clock support that small technical teams cannot sustain.
3. **Expert bottlenecks** — Over-reliance on senior technicians slows resolution times and creates single points of failure.
4. **Ignored manuals** — Customers bypass technical documentation and contact support directly for information already available.
5. **High operational costs** — Manual, inefficient workflows inflate the cost of every support interaction.
6. **Knowledge fragmentation** — Critical know-how is dispersed across emails, documents, and individual experts — never centralized.

## Features

### Primary Features

1. **Intelligent Request Routing**
   Automatically classifies and routes incoming tickets to the appropriate AI system or human specialist based on content, urgency, and customer profile.

2. **Management of Simple & Incomplete Requests**
   Detects routine or incomplete requests and responds automatically — asking for missing information or providing direct answers — without human intervention.

3. **Generative Answer Suggestions**
   For tickets requiring human review, AI generates draft responses sourced from technical manuals, past tickets, and the knowledge base. Technicians review and send with one click.

4. **Intelligent Knowledge Storage**
   Every resolved ticket enriches the knowledge base. The platform learns from technician interactions and transfers expert knowledge into a structured, searchable repository.

### Additional Features

5. **Ticket Prioritization**
   Tickets are automatically ranked by urgency, customer tier, warranty status, and estimated business impact.

6. **Customer & Machine Identification**
   The system recognizes customers and the specific machines they own, surfacing warranty status, interaction history, and machine documentation instantly.

7. **Automatic Translation**
   Real-time translation enables support across languages without requiring multilingual staff.
